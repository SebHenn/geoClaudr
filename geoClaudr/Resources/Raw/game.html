geoClaudr

Get dropped somewhere on Earth. Look around, then guess where you are.

The street imagery is powered by Mapillary (free). Paste your client token above — it's saved on this device. Get one in ~2 min: create an account at mapillary.com → Developers dashboard → Register Application → copy the token starting with MLY|.
Everyone who plays the same daily or seed gets the same 5 locations (fixed at World · 5 rounds · Move) — compare scores!

How to play

🌍 You're dropped on a random street somewhere in the world.

🖱️ Look around by dragging. Move by clicking the direction you want to go — or use W A S D on a keyboard.

🧭 The compass shows where north is. Tap ↩ Return to jump back to the drop, or 🔄 New spot if a place won't load.

🗺️ Open the map (bottom-right), click to drop your pin, fine-tune it, then hit GUESS.

🏆 The closer your pin, the more points — up to 5000 per round.
